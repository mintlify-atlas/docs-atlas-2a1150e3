TransactApp is a Windows desktop application that helps you track income, expenses, and cash inventory — all stored locally on your device using SQLite, with no internet connection required. Whether you're managing day-to-day spending or monitoring your cash on hand, TransactApp gives you a clear picture of your personal finances.

## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download TransactApp">
    Visit the [installation page](/installation) and choose between the ClickOnce installer (recommended for Microsoft Edge) or the standalone `setup.exe` (recommended for Chrome and Firefox).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and launch">
    Run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts. TransactApp will create a desktop shortcut and launch automatically after installation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record your first transaction">
    Open TransactApp and add your first income or expense entry. Set the amount, category, and date to start building your financial history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your finances">
    Use the reports view to see summaries of your spending and income, and check your cash inventory balance at any time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why TransactApp?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Offline and private" icon="shield-halved">
    All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your computer. No cloud sync, no accounts required — your financial data stays on your device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cash inventory tracking" icon="coins">
    Beyond basic expense tracking, TransactApp lets you monitor physical cash on hand — useful for budgeting with cash-based spending.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automatic updates" icon="rotate">
    TransactApp uses ClickOnce deployment for seamless automatic updates. When a new version is available, the app updates itself before launching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lightweight and fast" icon="bolt">
    Built on .NET with a small footprint, TransactApp starts quickly and runs efficiently without consuming significant system resources.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  TransactApp is a Windows desktop application and requires Windows with .NET runtime. See [system requirements](/reference/system-requirements) for full details.
</Note>
