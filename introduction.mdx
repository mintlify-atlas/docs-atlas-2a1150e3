TransactApp is a Windows desktop application designed for individuals who want full control over their personal finances without relying on cloud services or subscriptions. It stores all data locally in a SQLite database on your machine, keeping your financial information private and accessible offline.

## What TransactApp does

TransactApp helps you track where your money goes and how much cash you have on hand. The core workflow is straightforward: record income and expense transactions, monitor your cash inventory across different denominations, and generate reports to understand your financial picture over time.

Key capabilities:

- **Transaction tracking** — Log income and expense entries with dates, categories, and notes.
- **Cash inventory control** — Record physical cash by denomination and get running totals.
- **Local SQLite storage** — All data stays on your device. No account required, no internet connection needed for day-to-day use.
- **Automatic updates** — ClickOnce deployment checks for new versions before the app starts, so you always run the latest release without manual downloads.

## Who this is for

TransactApp is built for personal finance users who prefer a desktop application over a web app or mobile app. It is a good fit if you:

- Want your financial data stored locally, not on a third-party server.
- Manage physical cash and need to track bills and coins by denomination.
- Use a Windows PC as your primary device.
- Prefer a lightweight, install-once application with silent automatic updates.
